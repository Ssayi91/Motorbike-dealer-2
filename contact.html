Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your service center hours?

Our service center is open Monday to Friday from 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Do I need an appointment for service?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure we can serve you promptly. You can book an appointment online or by calling our service department.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), mobile payments (M-Pesa), bank transfers, and cash. Financing options are also available for motorcycle purchases.

Do you offer test rides?

Yes, we offer test rides for most models. Please bring your valid motorcycle license and proper riding gear. Contact our sales department to schedule a test ride appointment.

How often should I service my motorcycle?

Service intervals vary by make and model, but generally we recommend a basic service every 3,000-5,000 km and a full service every 10,000 km. Refer to your owner's manual or contact our service department for specific recommendations.
