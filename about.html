Where Racing Spirit Meets Community

For over 15 years, MotorBike World has been fueling Kenya's passion for motorcycles through racing sponsorships, community events, and a commitment to sportsmanship.

Our Racing Heritage

Founded in 2008 by former professional racer John Kamau, MotorBike World was born from a passion for competitive motorcycling and a desire to build Kenya's racing community.

What began as a small repair shop has evolved into a powerhouse supporting local racing talent and hosting national events. We've sponsored over 200 riders and organized 45 racing events across Kenya.

Our commitment to sportsmanship drives everything we do - from the track to the showroom. We believe racing builds character, discipline, and community.
